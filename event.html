About

ENYware is the brainchild of three developers who wanted to simplify the process of finding something to do. Using the Eventbrite, Weatherbit, and Yelp APIs, as well as the Algolia CDN, ENYware makes the process as simple as 1 2 3.

Want to see if there's anything exciting happening nearby? Just type in your city, and feel free to narrow it down with an event type. Wondering if you should break out that tank top or a light jacket? Each event comes loaded with specific weather info. Feeling peckish? Each event page comes with a few nearby restaurants.

Find things to do wherever you are, with Events Near You, Anywhere .
